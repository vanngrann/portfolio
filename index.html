About

As a bilingual computer science graduate with over six years of teaching experience, I am honored to work at one of the nation's top-ranked private schools. My experience at this prestigious institution has further refined my approach to fostering engaging, dynamic, and inclusive learning environments. While I am proud of the recognition of the school, my teaching methods are a reflection of my personal dedication to helping students excel in programming and technical subjects.


I bring expertise in both front-end and back-end web development, object-oriented programming (OOP), and the Flask framework, integrating industry-standard tools such as VSCode and GitHub. Over eight years of hands-on programming experience have equipped me to break down complex concepts into accessible lessons tailored to the needs of my students.


Currently, I specialize in tutoring introductory Python and offering custom Virtual Reality (VR) development services, helping clients bring their ideas to life through immersive VR experiences. Whether you're beginning your journey in programming or need guidance on a VR project, I am here to support your learning and development.
